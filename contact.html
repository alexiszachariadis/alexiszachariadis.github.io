Alexis Zachariadis

Alexis Zachariadis is an architect based in Stockholm, Sweden. He recently graduated from the KTH School of Architecture in Stockholm. He presented Polysporous Varvakios as his diploma project in June 2014.

A driving force for him is to find, within the architectural process, means of expressing a conceptual rigour and anchoring that in the context at hand. His conviction is that if the idea is allowed to encompass the architecture as a whole, it will be transmitted upon the user and influence her experience of the built environment in a profound way. It will lead to an engaging and all-encompassing architecture that adresses all scales at once. In that way, there emerges a dynamic relationship between small and big, between the built environment and the user, where they are contrasting, challenging and completing each other - a reciprocal relationship in constant motion.
